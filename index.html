About MusicMuse

MusicMuse is a simple and user-friendly MP3 music player that allows you to enjoy your favorite music with ease. Whether you're at home or on the go, MusicMuse provides a seamless music listening experience.

Key Features

  • Play and pause your music
  • Navigate through your music playlist
  • Go forward and backward between tracks
